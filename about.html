關於我們 (About Us)

「食材保鮮指南」的誕生，源自一個簡單的信念：減少食物浪費，從了解每一樣食材開始。我們致力於提供一個快速、準確且易於使用的查詢工具，幫助全球的家庭廚師與餐飲專業人士，以最正確的方式保存珍貴的食材。

The Food Storage Guide was born from a simple belief: reducing food waste starts with understanding our ingredients. We are dedicated to providing a fast, accurate, and user-friendly tool to help home cooks and culinary professionals worldwide store their valuable food items correctly.
